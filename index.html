About Us

At Ocean Corridor, we’re on a mission to bring the mysterious and mesmerizing world beneath the waves closer to you. Our website is dedicated to introducing people of all ages to the incredible creatures that inhabit our oceans — from the graceful jellyfish and intelligent octopus to the mighty whales and vibrant coral reefs.

Marine Life

Clownfish

Clownfish are orange-and-white reef fish that live with sea anemones. They’re immune to stings and help protect the anemone.

Axolotl

Axolotls are unique amphibians known for their ability to regenerate limbs and retain larval features throughout life. They live in freshwater lakes in Mexico and breathe through external feathery gills.

Sea Turtle

Sea turtles are graceful swimmers that journey across oceans. They play a vital role in marine ecosystems and face many conservation threats.

Corydoras

Corydoras are small, peaceful freshwater catfish commonly found in South American rivers. They are bottom dwellers that help keep tanks clean by scavenging leftover food.
